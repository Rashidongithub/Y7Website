Dogs

All about dogs

Dogs are very cute animals that interact with you and help support you emotionally. Dogs also can make you feel less lonely and bored so if you are sitting on the couch with nothing to do then you can simply play with your dog.

Why to buy a dog

  • They are active which means you will also have an active lifestyle.
  • They are so fun to play with
  • It seems like they can understand your feelings and what you are saying

Why not to buy a dog

  • Dogs can often shed which means you need to clean up their fur
  • Dogs grow very quickly so they might need a bigger cage
  • Lastly, dogs are a long term commitment and need a lot of resposibility
